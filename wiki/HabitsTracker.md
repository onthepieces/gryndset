# ⚡ Habits Tracker

Consistency is key for freelancers. The **Habits Tracker** allows you to outline daily routines, view Streaks, and audit consistency over the year.

---

## 🏃 Tracking Routines

* **Add Habits**: Assign name and category classification tags (e.g. `Health`, `Learning`, `Work`, `Routine`).
* **Interactive Checklist**: Toggle checkboxes on the habit card to mark tasks completed for today.
* **Streak Calculations**: Auto-calculates active Streaks by scanning consecutive days checked backwards from the current date. If you miss a day, the streak resets.

---

## 📅 Yearly Activity Matrix Grid

* The Habits Tracker displays a GitHub-style **365-day heat map calendar** for each habit.
* Days are represented by tiny grids.
* Checking off a habit colors the grid block, giving you an immediate visual summary of your consistency and monthly check-ins at a glance.
